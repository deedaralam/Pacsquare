About Us //
Rokivo is a digital experience design company with roots in Italy and San Francisco.

Pacsquare Technologies Pvt. Ltd. is a privately held software company based in Islamabad, Pakistan. The products and services offered by Pacsquare are primarily web based and desktop applications. Additionally, we provide services for iOS, Android, Blackberry

The overall range of products and services Pacsquare specializes in can be categorized into Web Development, Mobile Apps Development, Game Development, UI/UX, E Commerce Solutions, Desktop Development, Content writing and Management, Marketing Solutions and Trading Solutions.
